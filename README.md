Connextions

Connextions is a human-centered AI tool for managing real professional relationships.
It helps users remember, understand, and grow their network without turning it into a social feed.

This project explores how AI can support one-on-one professional relationships through memory, context, and semantic understanding — not likes, followers, or public profiles.

Problem

Professional relationships decay over time.

After events, meetings, and introductions, most people:

forget names, context, and follow-ups

lose track of who is relevant for what

rely on bloated platforms that prioritize visibility over memory

Existing tools (CRMs, LinkedIn, contact lists) optimize for broadcasting and metrics, not human recall or relational depth.

Core Idea

Connextions treats professional relationships like long-term memory, not social content.

Instead of asking:

“Who is the most popular or most recent?”

Connextions asks:

“Who is contextually relevant right now — and why?”

AI is used intentionally and conservatively to:

summarize relationships

preserve nuance and personal context

surface meaningful connections at the right moment

The goal is augmentation, not automation.

Key Principles

Human-centered AI
AI supports judgment — it does not replace it.

Not a social network
No feeds, no public profiles, no engagement farming.

Semantic over metric-based
Relevance is based on context and fit, not activity or popularity.

Ethical by design
No data selling. Transparent use of AI. User trust is a core feature.

Core Features (Planned / In Progress)

Connection profiles with:

semantic AI summaries

personal notes and memory cues

skills, experience, and trajectory

AI-assisted relevance scoring (non-absolute, user-guided)

Memory-style surfacing of connections for specific needs

Relationship tracking over time (growth, drift, changes)

Tiered AI capabilities depending on use case

Target User

Early versions of Connextions are designed for:

startup founders

students and early-career builders

professionals who meet many people and hate losing track of them

The platform prioritizes depth over scale.

Status

This repository is currently private and under active development.

The focus is on:

defining core data models

experimenting with AI reasoning approaches

validating product philosophy through iteration

The codebase is expected to change rapidly.
